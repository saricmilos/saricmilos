Understanding Solar Irradiance

Solar irradiance is the power per unit area received from the sun in the form of electromagnetic radiation. Key metrics include:

  • Global Horizontal Irradiance (GHI): Total solar radiation received on a horizontal surface
  • Direct Normal Irradiance (DNI): Solar radiation received directly from the sun
  • Diffuse Horizontal Irradiance (DHI): Solar radiation that is scattered by the atmosphere
